Frequently Asked Questions

We have given answers to the most popular questions below.

Is my crypto safe with this wallet?

Yes. We use industry-leading encryption and non-custodial storage so only you have access to your funds.

Which cryptocurrencies are supported?

We support Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, USDT, and many more. Our list is constantly updated as we add new chains and tokens.

How can I restore my wallet?

You can restore your wallet using your 12-word recovery phrase during setup.

Are there any fees for using the wallet?

Network fees apply when sending or swapping. We do not charge additional service fees.
